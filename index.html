About Audi

More than a century of innovation and progress

Founded in 1909, Audi has established itself as a premium manufacturer of automobiles that combine sporty performance, groundbreaking technology, and timeless design. The company's slogan "Vorsprung durch Technik" (Progress through Technology) reflects its commitment to innovation.

Today, Audi is at the forefront of the automotive industry's transformation, leading the charge in electric mobility, autonomous driving, and digital services while maintaining its reputation for exceptional craftsmanship and attention to detail.
